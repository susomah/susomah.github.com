Landmark project

Jackson Heights has its own meaning to each person who lives in this neighborhood. This place continously welcome new people of different ethnicity & culture and give them a space to be their ownselves. This place sometimes becomes a tourist spot, sometimes becomes hangout spot, sometimes becomes workplace, or sometimes home. The same way, Jackson Heights is a whole different "New York" to me. This is a feature article that I have written about my New York, "The Foreign City", to exhibit what Jackson Heights mean to me.
